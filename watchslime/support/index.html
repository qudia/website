Support

Q: What is WatchSlime?
A: WatchSlime is an Apple Watch-exclusive slime simulator app.

Q: What are the OS requirements?
A: WatchSlime supports watchOS 6.0 and up.

Q: How can I enable/disable haptic feedback?
A: Tap on the Haptic Feedback button in the bottom left corner of the main screen.

Q: How can I change the volume?
A: Choose a slime while Haptic Feedback is enabled and rotate the Digital Crown.
